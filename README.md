## Swiggy Sales Analysis Dashboard

An end-to-end Excel data analytics project analyzing sales performance for Swiggy — India's leading food delivery platform — covering orders across Veg and Non-Veg categories from restaurants spanning 28 cities and 28 states.

## Objective

Analyze Swiggy's order dataset to uncover actionable insights on sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance — helping the business optimize its delivery strategy and improve customer satisfaction.

## Dataset

1 consolidated dataset used in this project:
File | Records | Description
Swiggy_Raw_Data_Excel.xlsx | 197,430 | State, City, Order Date, Day, Quarter, Week, Restaurant Name, Location, Category, Dish Name, Food Type, Price (INR), Rating, Rating Count

## Key Insights

Bengaluru is the top revenue city, contributing Rs. 5.46M — over 10% of total revenue
Recommended category dominates product revenue at Rs. 7.19M (13.5% of total)
Revenue is stable month-on-month, ranging from Rs. 6.27M to Rs. 6.83M
Veg orders lead with 64.5% of total revenue (Rs. 34.18M)
Average rating held steady at 4.34 across all three quarters
Saturday is the highest revenue day; Tuesday is the lowest
